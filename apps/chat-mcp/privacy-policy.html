Privacy Policy for ChatMCP

Effective Date: 06/28/2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Kitsune Apps ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application, ChatMCP ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Initiated Data Access: The App accesses your device's local tools such as Calendars, Contacts, Mail, Reminders, and other iOS features only when you initiate such interactions.
  • LLM Provider Connections: The App enables you to connect with various language learning model (LLM) providers including Anthropic, Gemini, OpenAI, and others.
  • Firebase Analytics: We collect usage data through Firebase Analytics to help us understand how users interact with our App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: We use the data accessed from your local tools solely to provide the functionality you request within the App.
  • Third-Party LLM Services: When you use the App to connect to LLM providers, data you retrieve from your local tools (such as calendar events, contacts, etc.) is sent to your chosen LLM provider to generate responses and process your requests.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us improve the App's functionality and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Retention

ChatMCP does not store any user data on our servers. The App operates as an interface that facilitates communication between:

  • Your device's local tools
  • Your chosen LLM providers
  • MCP servers (when applicable)

Any data processed remains temporary and is not retained by us after your session ends.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • LLM Providers: When you use the App to interact with LLM providers (Anthropic, Gemini, OpenAI, etc.), the data you choose to share, including content retrieved from your local tools, is transmitted to these third-party services. This transmission is necessary for the functionality you request.
  • MCP Servers: If you utilize MCP server connections, data will be transmitted to these servers according to their own privacy practices.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Third-Party Privacy Policies

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party LLM providers or services that you connect through our App. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you use through ChatMCP:

  • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google (Gemini): https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

6. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect usage data that helps us improve the App. This may include:

  • App usage statistics
  • Device information
  • Feature usage patterns

This data is anonymized and used solely for improving our services and understanding user behavior.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Your Choices

You can control what information is accessed by:

  • Managing iOS system permissions for the App
  • Choosing which LLM providers to connect with
  • Controlling which local tools you allow the App to access

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
